# Finance & Accounting

```
Sales & Receivables
Trigger
Customer transaction occurs or payment activity happens

Input
Internal: ERP customer master data, product master data, pricing tables, tax rules, past sales orders
External: Customer emails, purchase orders, payment confirmations, bank statements

Output
Sales invoice issued, receipt recorded, customer balance updated, cash position updated

Feedback
Invoice disputes, late payment detection, payment mismatch, currency fluctuation variance, customer credit risk changes


---

Core Accounting
Trigger
Financial event recorded

Input
Internal: General ledger, chart of accounts, prior journal entries, accounting policies
External: Scanned receipts, contracts, handwritten adjustments

Output
General ledger updated, financial position recorded, adjusted balances

Feedback
Imbalanced ledger, policy misalignment, incorrect categorization, audit findings


---

Control & Reconciliation
Trigger
Periodic review or anomaly detection

Input
Internal: Transaction logs, approval workflows, system audit logs
External: Bank statements, scanned error reports, emails reporting exceptions

Output
Reconciled accounts, risk reports, exception logs

Feedback
Detected inconsistencies, fraud indicators, control weaknesses


---

Performance Analysis
Trigger
Reporting cycle or management request

Input
Internal: Ledger balances, cost data, revenue data, department allocations, KPIs
External: Analyst commentary, management notes

Output
Performance reports, profitability insights, KPI dashboards

Feedback
Performance decline, cost overrun, revenue underperformance


---

Planning & Forecasting
Trigger
Planning cycle start or strategic review

Input
Internal: Historical expense and revenue data, financial models, scenario parameters
External: Market outlook reports, analyst assumptions, strategic plans

Output
Approved budgets, forecast models, investment plans

Feedback
Deviation from actual results, scenario failure, cash constraints


---

Tax & Compliance
Trigger
Regulatory deadlines or tax period

Input
Internal: Financial transactions, tax code tables, statutory report templates
External: Legal memos, government notices, scanned tax forms

Output
Tax filings, compliance reports, audit packages

Feedback
Regulatory changes, audit adjustments, penalty warnings


---

Asset & Liability Management
Trigger
Asset acquisition or liability creation

Input
Internal: Asset registers, depreciation schedules, loan records
External: Lease agreements, loan contracts, asset appraisals

Output
Asset records, depreciation schedules, debt amortization plans

Feedback
Asset impairment, interest fluctuation, contract renegotiation


---

Capital & Strategy Finance
Trigger
Strategic investment decision

Input
Internal: Risk/return models, funding constraints, financial projections
External: Market reports, analyst notes

Output
Capital allocation models, funding strategies, pricing simulations

Feedback
Investment underperformance, market volatility, strategic misalignment


---

Reporting & Analytics
Trigger
Reporting request or monitoring cycle

Input
Internal: Aggregated financial data, KPIs, operational metrics, dashboards
External: Analyst commentary, meeting notes

Output
Dashboards, executive reports, analytics summaries

Feedback
User behavior, report relevance, business direction changes


---

Treasury & Risk
Trigger
Liquidity change or currency exposure

Input
Internal: Cash flow data, debt profiles, liquidity positions
External: FX rates, market commentary, analyst emails

Output
Risk mitigation strategy, liquidity plans, hedging actions

Feedback
Market shifts, liquidity stress, interest movement


---

Group Consolidation
Trigger
Period-end close

Input
Internal: Subsidiary financial statements, intercompany transactions, transfer pricing tables
External: Subsidiary notes, intercompany memos

Output
Consolidated financial statements

Feedback
Elimination errors, currency distortion, compliance variance


---

Audit & Governance
Trigger
Audit cycle or investigation start

Input
Internal: Transaction histories, control framework data, compliance logs
External: Internal reports, whistleblower notes

Output
Audit reports, risk exposure findings

Feedback
Control failures, governance gaps, policy revisions


---

Corporate Finance & M&A
Trigger
Strategic expansion or restructuring decision

Input
Internal: Valuation data, financial statements, investment projections
External: Market analysis, due diligence notes, contracts

Output
M&A strategy, IPO readiness reports, capital restructuring plans

Feedback
Deal inefficiencies, valuation mismatches, market response


---

International Tax & Cross-Border
Trigger
Cross-border transaction or expansion

Input
Internal: International tax codes, global transaction records
External: Legal memos, regulatory guidance

Output
Tax strategy, compliance structure

Feedback
Legal changes, penalty risk, strategic tax adjustment
```
